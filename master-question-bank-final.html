5. Mathematics

Core Concepts
∫

Calculus

  • Explain derivative as rate of change with a real-life example.
  • What does a definite integral represent geometrically?
  • Find maxima/minima of f(x)=x³-3x² — explain steps.
  • Why is lim (sin x)/x = 1 as x→0 important?
  • Differentiate eˣ ln x — which rules apply?
Σ

Algebra

  • Solve: 2x+3y=7, x-y=1 — explain your method.
  • What are eigenvalues intuitively? Where used in data?
  • Differentiate linear vs quadratic growth with examples.
  • What is rank of a matrix and why does it matter?
  • If log₂8 = x, solve mentally and explain logs simply.
%

Probability

  • Probability vs odds — explain with a coin toss.
  • State Bayes' Theorem in plain English.
  • Bag: 3 red, 2 blue — P(2 reds without replacement)?
  • Define expectation and variance intuitively.
  • What is Central Limit Theorem and why do businesses care?
∞

Sequences

  • Find nth term of AP: 5, 9, 13, 17...
  • AP vs GP — give one business example each.
  • Sum of infinite GP: 1 + 1/2 + 1/4 + ... — explain.
  • Fibonacci sequence — one real-world application.
  • How do sequences relate to compound interest?

7. Interview Etiquette

9 Points
01 • Be Early Join 5 mins early, camera at eye level, plain background, good lighting.
02 • Dress Sharp Formal even online — solid light colors work best on camera.
03 • Greet Well "Good morning panelists" — wait to be asked to sit.
04 • Listen First Let them finish. Pause 1 sec before answering.
05 • Structure Use PEL: Point → Example → Learning. Keep to 60-90s.
06 • Honesty "I'm not certain, but I'd approach it as..." beats bluffing.
07 • Energy Smile, sit forward, eye contact with all panelists.
08 • Notes Keep a neat notepad; jot keywords, not scripts.
09 • Close Smart Ask about iMBA pedagogy or labs — not placements.
