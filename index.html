Greetings!

A message for prospective IMBA candidates.

IMBA Beacon is positioned as a focused preparation initiative to help students approach the IIT Mandi IMBA interview with clarity, confidence, and structure. The goal is not just practice, but a complete preparation ecosystem that mirrors the seriousness of an institutional platform.

With mock interviews, communication support, live classes, practice sheets, and interaction sessions, the platform is designed to help aspirants understand both the interview process and their own story more deeply.
