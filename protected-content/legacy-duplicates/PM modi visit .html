PM Modi's 5-Nation Tour May 2026: Strategic Deep Dive for India

Energy Security, Semiconductors, and the New Europe-Gulf Axis
SCENARIO ANALYSIS: This is a forward-looking hypothetical assessment based on policy trends as of 2025-26. All outcomes, figures, and MoUs listed are projected scenarios for planning purposes, not confirmed government announcements.

Executive Summary

  • Energy Hedge: Amid Strait of Hormuz volatility in Q2 2026, the UAE and Norway legs are projected to lock in 10-year strategic oil storage and LNG diversification, reducing India's Gulf choke-point exposure from 62% to under 50% by 2028.
  • Chip Sovereignty: The Netherlands-Sweden corridor targets India's $10B semiconductor mission with projected ASML training hub, Tata Electronics packaging JV, and Ericsson 6G testbeds in Bengaluru and Chennai.
  • New Defence Axis: A projected India-UAE Strategic Defence Partnership, plus deeper Saab and Leonardo co-production talks, signals a shift from buyer-seller to joint R&D across drones, maritime domain awareness, and green steel for defence.
  • IMEC Revival: With Italy as host of the G7 outreach, the tour is expected to reboot the India-Middle East-Europe Economic Corridor with concrete financing from UAE sovereign funds and EU gateway ports (Rotterdam, Trieste).
LEG 1 • UAE

United Arab Emirates – Abu Dhabi (May 15)

Context: Hormuz Crisis

Following April 2026 drone incidents near the Strait of Hormuz, Brent spiked to $98/bbl and insurance premiums for Indian tankers rose 40%. India imports ~4.7 mbpd, with 62% transiting Hormuz. The UAE offers alternative pipelines (Habshan-Fujairah) bypassing the Strait.

Projected 7 Key Outcomes

  1. Strategic Petroleum Reserve Access: 10-year lease for 6 million barrels storage at Fujairah, with first-right-of-refusal for India.
  2. Rupee-Dirham 2.0: Expansion to cover 75% of non-oil trade ($42B), cutting transaction costs by $400M annually.
  3. ADNOC Long-Term LNG: 2.5 MTPA for 15 years starting 2027, priced at 11.5% slope to Brent.
  4. India-UAE Defence Partnership (IUDP): Formalised framework for joint maritime patrols in Arabian Sea, ISR sharing, and logistics access.
  5. IMEC Investment Commitment: ADIA and Mubadala projected $5B anchor investment into IMEC-linked infrastructure.
  6. Food Security Corridor: 250,000 acres in Gujarat and MP for UAE-financed millet and pulses farms for Gulf export.
  7. Critical Minerals: Joint venture for lithium processing in Abu Dhabi using Indian technology.

Strategic Defence Partnership Specifics

Expected to include: bi-annual 'Desert Seal' naval exercise, Indian Navy access to Fujairah for logistics, joint production of autonomous surface vessels with EDGE Group, and a real-time maritime domain awareness link between Gurugram IFC-IOR and Abu Dhabi.

Energy Impact Numbers

  • Additional 90 days of Hormuz-bypass cover for western India refineries.
  • LNG diversification: UAE share rises from 8% to 14% of India's LNG basket.
  • Projected $1.2B annual savings from rupee-dirham settlement by 2027.

Why Critical

The UAE is India's 3rd largest trade partner ($83.7B FY25) and home to 3.5M Indians. Securing Fujairah bypass capacity is the single biggest de-risking move for India's energy since the 1970s oil shocks.

TATA-ASML Details

Scenario anticipates Tata Electronics signing MoU with ASML and ASM International for a 3-year advanced lithography training program in Eindhoven for 500 Indian engineers, supporting Tata's Dholera fab ramp-up. No EUV tool transfer expected, but DUV immersion training and metrology IP access included.

Chola Bronze Plates Return

Dutch authorities are expected to formally return 12 Chola-era (12th century) bronze temple plates seized in 2019, now housed at Leiden Museum. This is projected as the largest single cultural repatriation from the Netherlands.

EU Gateway Role

Rotterdam is critical for IMEC's western node. Dutch pension fund APG ($550B AUM) is in talks for India infra debt – projected $2B allocation.

LEG 3 • SWEDEN

Sweden – Stockholm (May 17)

Honour Details

PM Modi is projected to be conferred the Royal Order of the Polar Star – Sweden's highest honour for foreigners, instituted 1748 by King Frederick I. If awarded, it would be his 31st international state honour, recognising India-Sweden innovation ties.

Bilateral Snapshot

Trade: $7.75B. Over 280 Swedish companies operate in India (IKEA, Volvo, Ericsson, Saab). Meetings expected with PM Ulf Kristersson and Crown Princess Victoria at Haga Palace.

Green Steel

SSAB and Tata Steel expected to sign HYBRIT hydrogen-based steel tech licensing for Jamshedpur pilot – targeting 1 MTPA green steel by 2030.

Ericsson 6G

Projected joint 6G testbed at IISc Bengaluru with $150M Ericsson R&D expansion, focusing on NTN (non-terrestrial networks) for rural India.

Saab Defence

Discussions on Gripen-E sensor co-development and Carl-Gustaf M4 production scale-up in India (already at 70% localisation).

Tagore Centenary

2026 marks 100 years of Rabindranath Tagore's 1926 Sweden visit. Expected joint stamp and Nobel-Tagore dialogue at Swedish Academy.

LEG 5 • ITALY

Italy – Rome (May 20)

IMEC Corridor Status

After 18-month pause due to Middle East conflict, Italy as IMEC co-chair is expected to push Trieste port upgrades and ETR rail link financing. Projected deliverable: Italy-India IMEC joint task force with €1B EIB funding window.

Meloni Meeting Expectations

Focus on Mediterranean-Indian Ocean security, migration, and defence industrial ties. Italy has invited India as G7 Outreach partner for third consecutive year.

Defence – Leonardo

Talks on co-production of AW-139 helicopters in India for Navy and Coast Guard, plus naval gun systems with BHEL.

Migration Pact

Expected legal mobility agreement for 15,000 Indian skilled workers annually in manufacturing, shipbuilding, aged care – modelled on Germany pact.

G7 Coordination

Italy to back India's push for Global South debt relief framework and AI governance at June 2026 G7 in Florence.

Risks and Watchpoints

Execution Risk: 17 Dutch MoUs are mostly non-binding; ASML training depends on EU export licences.
Hormuz Escalation: If conflict widens, Fujairah capacity may be insufficient for full diversification.
EU Politics: Dutch and Swedish coalition governments face elections in 2027 – continuity risk.
IMEC Financing: $5B UAE pledge contingent on Israel-Saudi normalisation progress.
Domestic Optics: Opposition may question honour-chasing vs domestic jobs impact.
Technology Transfer: No EUV tools – India still 2 generations behind Taiwan/Korea in leading edge.
